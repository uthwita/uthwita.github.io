About

Uþwita is an old English Word that means scholar. Our printers mark (see abouve) is Bede the Venerable imposed on an old German heraldry shield. Bede, an English monk & scholar born c. 673, is known as the father of English history.

Uþwita Press publishes high quality books that center around history, philosophy and science. Its mission is to provide dissident scholarly books in a world where academia is dead. Audacia pro muro et scuto opus.
